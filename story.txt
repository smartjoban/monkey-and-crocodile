story
once upon a time in a forst there lived a monkey who stayed on a berry tree, which was on the banks of the river. in the same forest, there lived a crocodile and his wife.
one day, the crocodile came to the banks of the river and rested under a tree. The kind hearted monkey offered him some fruits. The crocodile came back the next day for 
more fruits. As the days pass, the crocodile and the monkey became friends
